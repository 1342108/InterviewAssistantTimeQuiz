About Interview Assistant

Our application is focused purely on assisting those who need help when preparing for interviews. Interview Assistant is an application that will help you practice answering common and complex interview questions, whilst also providing example answers and tips. Interview Assistant stands out to similar applications in the same market because of the features we have. Quizzes and videos are included in this application to help improve your knowledge and confidence. Finally the feature that makes us unique and different is our Visual Practice feature, where you have the opportunity to test how much you've learnt by having a practice interview, that will help you get that dream job.

Visual Practice

This is the most unique feature of Interview Assistant, which makes us stand out to any other application in the market. The Visual Practice feature gives user's the opportunity to have a practice interview with a character of their own choice. Interview questions will be asked and feedback will be given after. Therefore it's cruical user's maintain they're eye contact at all times as well as speak loud enough and clear enough to recieve good feedback and to help prepare them for their actual interview.
